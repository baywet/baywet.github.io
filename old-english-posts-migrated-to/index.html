Old English posts migrated to this blog

Nov 1, 2016

Everything is in the title. My French blog has been running for more than six years now (gee, I’m getting old).

From 2013 to 2014, before I decided to open an English dedicated blog, I was posting every blog entry in English and in French.

Over time it got messy for both communities so I decided to open this blog. But I couldn’t move the old content to this new blog without having to spend hours doing so.

We recently upgraded our platform and one of the new features (maybe it was there before but the administration interface used to be such a mess…) is to be able to move posts from one blog to another.

This content is mostly about SharePoint, .net and Entity framework.

Enjoy!
