Montreal SharePoint Saturday 2015

Mar 3, 2015

Montreal will have a SharePoint Saturday this year too!

Just as a reminder SharePoint Saturdays are:

  • Free events
  • Happening all around the world
  • Dedicated to SharePoint/Office 365
  • On Saturdays
  • With great sessions and awesome speakers

Practical information

This year’s edition will be on May the 23rd at “college maisonneuse”. You just have to register here https://www.spsevents.org/city/montreal/montreal2015

Last year the SPS MTL brought together 200 persons. This year will try to have even more people with 4 tracks (20 sessions total).

You’d like to give a hand?

We are looking for volunteers to help organize this day (welcoming attendees, coats check, helping speakers…) Just send an e-mail to Sps@sharepointquebec.org

Fancy speaking?

If you have a specific knowledge on SharePoint/Office 365 you’d like to share during this day, go ahead and submit a session:

https://www.spsevents.org/city/Montreal/Montreal2015/_layouts/15/SPSEvents/Speakers/SessionForm.aspx

A great business opportunity

It is also a great opportunity for your business if you a working around SharePoint (consulting, services, products…). You’ll be able to reach a very targeted audience for a little cost. Feel free to send the information to your marketing department.

https://www.spsevents.org/city/Montreal/Montreal2015/_layouts/15/SPSEvents/Sponsors/SponsorForm.aspx

Social networks

Use the #SPSMTL hashtag

Go register now and see you there!
